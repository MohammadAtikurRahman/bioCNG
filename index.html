Our Mission

To Transform waste into the valuable resources, focusing on sustainability, innovation, research and reducing environmental impact. Effat BioCNG Ltd. aims to expand its operations beyond Bangladesh, Targeting markets in Europe and beyond.

Our Vision

Technology and Process

1. Waste Collection

Effat BioCNG Ltd. gathers municipal solid waste (MSW) and organic residues for biogas production.

Find Out More

2. Organic Separation

Collected waste is sorted to separate organic materials, ensuring only suitable residues proceed

Find Out More

3. Anaerobic Digestion

Separated organics undergo anaerobic digestion, converting them into raw biogas in an oxygen-free environment

Find Out More

4. Biogas Purification

Raw biogas is purified to remove impurities, ensuring it meets quality standards for the BioCNG conversion

Find Out More

5. BioCNG Production

Purified biogas is compressed into BioCNG, offering a renewable, economically viable, and eco-friendly fuel source

Find Out More
